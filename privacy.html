Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Millwright Engineer website (millwrightengineer.com) and the Ferrum Kragos Discord bot, both operated by Millwright Engineer, handle information.

1. The Website

This website is a static informational site. It does not require an account, does not run user tracking scripts beyond what may be provided by the hosting provider (currently GitHub Pages), and does not sell personal information. Standard server logs maintained by the hosting provider may include IP addresses, request times, and user-agent strings.

When you contact Millwright Engineer by email or phone using information on this site, the message and any details you choose to share are used only to respond to your inquiry.

2. Ferrum Kragos (Discord Bot)

Ferrum Kragos is an OpenAI-powered Discord bot operated by Millwright Engineer. The following sections describe how Ferrum Kragos handles information.

2.1 Information Collected

Ferrum Kragos may process messages, usernames, server IDs, channel IDs, and other metadata necessary to operate and respond to users.

2.2 How Information Is Used

Information may be used to respond to requests, operate the service, troubleshoot issues, maintain functionality, and improve Ferrum Kragos’ performance.

2.3 Powered by OpenAI

Ferrum Kragos uses OpenAI-powered systems to process prompts and generate responses. Information submitted to Ferrum Kragos may be transmitted to and processed by OpenAI or other service providers required to operate the service.

2.4 Data Sharing

Information is not sold. Data may be shared with third-party service providers only as needed to operate, host, support, secure, or improve Ferrum Kragos and related services.

2.5 Data Retention

Data is retained only as long as reasonably necessary to operate, maintain, and troubleshoot the service, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

3. Security

Reasonable efforts are made to protect information, but no method of storage, transmission, or processing can be guaranteed completely secure.

4. User Rights

Users may contact Millwright Engineer with privacy-related questions or requests concerning the website or Ferrum Kragos.
